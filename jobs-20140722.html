Starter jobs reality

Here are a couple of jobs in South Brooklyn. We are interviewing in East Flatbush so these jobs are reachable via either the B68 or the Q line subway.

BJ's

BJ's is a large chain of retail warehouses. They are opening soon in Brooklyn and have to hire a lot of people. The application site is at 1218 Neptune Ave (near W12th street).

Rocco's

This is a pizzeria. The sign is in Spanish. It means they are looking for someone to wash dishes. It is in the shopping center at West 5th and Neptune Ave.
