Nineth Avenue Jobs

Published: Sun 20 July 2014
By emisshula

In Starter-jobs.

tags: jobs reentry

To make our way, we must have firm resolve, persistence, tenacity. We must gear ourselves to work hard all the way. We can never let up. Ralph Bunche, grandson of a slave and Nobel prize winner

Starter jobs reality

This week is a mix. Tomorrow I will have a bunch of jobs in South Brooklyn. Today, these are jobs on the West Side near where I work.

Roast Kitchen

This is an expanding mid price restaurant chain. I saw the sign in the village at one of their construction sites. I love that they put real people to call in the advertisement. (Ellie Karagiannis and Giselle Jaramillo, can be reached at (212) 664-1820.) As always, I would go to their location rather than call. Their main location is at 49th and 7th ave. The address is: 740 7th Ave, New York, NY 10019

Holey Cream

Who doesn't wnat to sell ice cream during the summer. Although, the adverticement gives an email address to send a resume, I would strongly urge you to go. The address is 796 9th Ave (at 53rd Street).

Pita Kitchen

This is a delivery job. You can make good money in tips. The address is 790 9th Ave (at 52nd Street) New York, NY 10019
