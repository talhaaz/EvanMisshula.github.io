Today in bullshit

I was unemployed for a long time. I was a job developer for six months at Exodus Transitional Community in 2009 after I came home. Sometimes everyone needs a bullshit job. These are not a career. If you work at Popeye's you are not going to own Popeye's chicken. However there is a human dignity to work that may allow you to buy your kid a slice of pizza. Every dad deserves that.
