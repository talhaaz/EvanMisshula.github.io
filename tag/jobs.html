Brighton Ditmas Park

Published: Mon 28 July 2014
By emisshula

In Starter-jobs.

tags: jobs reentry

A man's bread and butter is only insured when he works for it. ~Marcus Garvey, a Jamaican political leader, publisher, journalist, entrepreneur, and orator.

Getting to the job

More jobs in South Brooklyn. The first three are in Brighton Beach. You can get there via the B or Q line or the B68 bus. The last two are near Newkirk Plaza in Ditmas Park.

Gourmet Deli

This is a large deli/small supermarket that opened in Brighton beach since Hurricane Sandy. It serves the area' growing latin and Bangladeshi communities. It is next to 3057 Coney Island Avenue on the corner of West 10th Street Court, one block south of Neptune ave.

Tacos El Rey

Mexican restaurant in Brighton Beach. They are looking for a waiter. This is a busy cash only place. The food is ok at best but they are the only restaurant in the are so they are always busy. The address is 3168 Coney Island Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11235.

Royal Pizza

This is the best pizza place in Brighton Beach. Completely remodeled since Hurrican Sandy. The address is 705 Brighton Beach Ave #1, New York, NY 11235.

One stop camera

This place was holding interviews yesterday. The address is 1100 Coney Island Ave. It is in Ditmas Park near the Newkirk Ave stop. It is between Foster Ave and Avenue H.

Pearland Bokerage/AllTaxi

Bunch of help wanted signs for drivers here. This is on the corner of Foster Ave and Coney Island Ave. The address is 1043 Coney Island Ave.
