Starter jobs reality

In NYC a lot starter jobs are in food service or retail. I know that is not for everyone. It is hard work. Most of the time though, being unemployed is worse.

Lenny's

Roslyn

This is a high end women's retial store. The sign was in the store when I walked to work yesterday. The address is 276 Columbus Ave. I don't know anything about the place. If they hire you, I will tell my girlfriend to shop there.
