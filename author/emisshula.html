Starter jobs today

I was unemployed for a long time. I was a job developer for six months at Exodus Transitional Community in 2009 after I came home. Sometimes everyone needs a starter job or a bullshit job. These are not a career. If you work at Popeye's you are not going to own Popeye's chicken. However there is a human dignity to work. Working may allow you to buy your kid a slice of pizza. Every dad deserves that. Here are the jobs I saw today.

Empanada Mama

This is a small but really busy place. They need people go here first. The address is 763 9th Ave, New York, NY 10019:

Hummus kitchen

This is a kosher middle eastern place. I hope that they remember people have been throwing Jews into jails since they invented prison. The address is 416 Amsterdam Ave (80th street), New York, NY 10024.

Kebab house

I know nothing except they had a sign out. If they hire you, I will eat there, 712 9th Ave:
